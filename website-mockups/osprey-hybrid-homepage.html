Questions

Before you ask

Which sports does Osprey coach?

Nine, each on its own blueprint: running, ultra, cycling, swimming, rowing, triathlon, powerlifting, HYROX, and CrossFit. One engine drives them all, so your lifting and your endurance work never contradict each other.

Do I need a wearable?

No. Osprey reads HRV and sleep from a watch or ring if you have one, but bar speed, pace drift, and your own session notes carry the signal on their own.

How does Osprey decide when I deload?

Deloads are planned into your season from day one — roughly every fourth week. The signals move one forward or soften a week early when your body disagrees with the calendar. You always see which signals triggered the change.

Can I use it alongside a human coach?

Yes. Export any week, share your season view, or let your coach adjust sessions directly — Osprey keeps the signal-watching either way.
