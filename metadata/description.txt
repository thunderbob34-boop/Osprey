OSPREY is the training companion built for hybrid athletes — people who run miles, pull heavy, and refuse to choose a lane. Whether you're logging a long run at sunrise, grinding through a strength session at lunch, or piecing together a race-week taper, OSPREY tracks everything and adapts around you.

At the center of it all is Ozzie, your AI coach. Ozzie isn't a chatbot with a clipboard. He shows up in your earpiece mid-run with live pacing cues, checks in on your recovery, adjusts your nutrition targets when your weight trend shifts, and gives you a daily brief each morning so you know exactly what you're walking into. He's warm, direct, occasionally goofy, and always in your corner.

WHAT OSPREY DOES

GPS Run Tracking with Live Coaching
Track every run with full GPS routing, real-time pace, heart rate zones, and elevation. Ozzie reads your splits as they happen and adjusts coaching cues on the fly — not after the fact.

Strength Logging
Log every lift with set-by-set tracking, rest timers, and volume history. OSPREY tracks progressive overload across training blocks so Ozzie can actually see whether you're improving.

Swim, Bike, and Cross Training
Full support for swim sessions, cycling, and cross training — not just a notes field. Distance, duration, perceived effort, and TSS all feed into your performance analytics.

AI Daily Brief
Every morning, Ozzie synthesizes your recent training load, recovery metrics, sleep trends, and what's on your schedule to give you a honest, personalized read on the day ahead. No guessing whether to go hard or dial it back.

Food Logging
Scan barcodes, photograph a meal, or type it in. OSPREY tracks macros against your targets and Ozzie nudges you when your protein is running low or your carbs aren't matching your training day.

Training Plan Generation
Tell Ozzie your goal, your race date, and how many days a week you can train. He builds a periodized plan that blends strength and endurance — not two separate programs duct-taped together.

Performance Analytics
OSPREY calculates ATL, CTL, and TSB (form, fitness, and fatigue) the way endurance coaches have done it for decades. Watch your fitness build across a training block and know when you're primed to race.

Race Hub
Add races to your calendar and OSPREY builds a race file: goal pace, logistics notes, packing list, and post-race retrospective. After the race, log how it went and Ozzie uses it to inform the next training block.

Group Challenges
Compete with friends on weekly mileage, lifting volume, or consistency streaks. Challenges sync automatically — no manual reporting, no honor system.
